The Opportunity

Every citizen touchpoint is a service. Every service is the nation.

Singapore's public sector organisations manage complex ecosystems spanning defence logistics, enterprise development, housing, social services, and wildlife conservation. Citizens and workers interact with dozens of disconnected platforms, each with its own logic. The opportunity is to unify these touchpoints into intelligent, people-centred experiences where the service navigates to the citizen, not the other way around.

AI-powered platforms replace static portals with conversational citizen services, policy intelligence, and connected workspaces. Transport workflows gain transparency across roles. Enterprise support becomes self-service. Housing journeys start from user insight. Knowledge flows are personalised and unified. The interface shifts from navigating government structure to government understanding and anticipating the needs of the people it serves.

AKQA builds the experience layer that connects public institutions to the people they serve. Design systems create consistency across agencies. Martech ecosystems enable data sharing between departments. Multilingual, accessible, and privacy-first by design, every platform is built sovereign and secure — ready for the scale and sensitivity that public service demands.

What We Do
01
Connected Citizen Services
HDB built a digital ecosystem that guides home buyers and owners through property journeys with transparency and clarity. The platform connects citizens to information, to services, and to the nation's housing mission — intelligent, accessible, and designed for life at every stage.
02
Enterprise Growth Ecosystems
Enterprise Singapore partnered with AKQA to serve 290,000 businesses through an integrated website and MarTech ecosystem. The platform reduced friction for business owners, enabled self-service support, and created data sharing between departments — making it easier for enterprises to access the guidance they need to grow.
03
Transport Management Systems
MINDEF needed a unified platform to manage vehicle acquisition, assignment, and tracking across military operations. AKQA partnered with ST Engineering and SMRT to design a system that replaced manual processes with mobile-optimised coordination, giving soldiers and operations staff transparency and efficiency in every indent.
04
Connected Workspaces
Temasek partnered with AKQA to build a connected workspace platform that unifies knowledge sharing and collaboration across the organisation. The system integrates third-party workplace applications, surfaces personalised actions, and creates a single source for institutional knowledge and daily workflows.
05
People-Centred Service Platforms
NTUC represents 1.5 million workers and members across Singapore. AKQA redesigned their entire experience platform — from communications playbooks to public-facing services — creating a consistent identity that works for millions. Every touchpoint reflects NTUC's commitment to worker welfare and dignity.

Case Studies
MINDEF

World-class transportation management for national defence readiness

AKQA partnered with ST Engineering and SMRT to design and optimise MINDEF's transport management platform. The work created a one-stop system with coordination and visibility across roles, enabling soldiers and operations staff to process indents and complete tasks through mobile-optimised, transparent workflows.
Enterprise Singapore

Inspiring businesses to grow beyond their current capabilities

AKQA built an integrated Martech ecosystem and redesigned Enterprise Singapore's website to reduce friction for business owners seeking support. The platform enabled internal data sharing between departments and delivered self-service tools that made it easier for the agency to guide enterprises on their growth journey.
NTUC

A consistent platform for every Singapore worker

AKQA designed an end-to-end experience platform representing NTUC's values across multiple touchpoints and channels. The work delivered an inclusive front-stage for 1.5 million workers and members, an operationally efficient back-stage, and a communications playbook empowering internal and external teams.
