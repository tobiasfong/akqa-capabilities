The Opportunity

Foundation models can now reason over a customer's full financial history, understand intent from natural language, and execute transactions across core banking systems in real time. This changes everything. The banking interface is no longer a screen — it is a conversation.

AI agents replace every static touchpoint: the FAQ page becomes a reasoning engine, the mortgage portal becomes a persistent advisor, onboarding becomes a single session that ends with an active account. Customers stop navigating the bank's structure. The bank navigates to them.

AKQA builds the AI-native experience layer. Tool servers connect agents directly to core banking, CRM, and compliance systems. Knowledge graphs power personalisation at the individual level. The result: a bank that acts before the customer asks.
