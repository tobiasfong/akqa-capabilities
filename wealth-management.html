The Opportunity

Wealth management across APAC spans a wide range — from Australian superannuation to private banking and first-time investors. Each needs different tools.

In Australia, a superannuation sector managing trillions in retirement savings faces a different design challenge to private banking in Singapore — but both share the same need: digital experiences that build trust, reach more members, and make complex financial decisions feel manageable. Australia's compulsory super system serves millions of workers, yet most funds have historically delivered advice to under 1% of their membership. AKQA is helping close that gap.

For HESTA, we built Future Planner — an interactive online retirement planning tool that expanded advice-reach from under 1% to 98.5% of the fund's 700,000+ members. For UOB, we built a wealth ecosystem with front-stage tools for clients and backstage tools for bankers across ASEAN. For Bank of Singapore, we designed RM navigator and pitchbook tools for private bank advisory. And for MIDF and Nanovest, mobile investment apps aimed at everyday investors in Malaysia and Indonesia.

What connects all of this is AKQA's approach: we start by understanding how people actually invest and how advisors actually work, then design and build platforms that fit those real behaviours.

Case Studies
UOB

Wealth ecosystem connecting clients and bankers across ASEAN

AKQA delivered an ecosystem of front-stage solutions for wealth clients and backstage tools for relationship managers across UOB's ASEAN network. The suite of digital solutions enables the bank to scale advisory services while continuously optimising client relationships and portfolio performance.
Bank of Singapore

Private banking platform for high-net-worth client relationships

AKQA worked closely with Bank of Singapore to understand how high-net-worth individuals with banking relationships between two and two hundred million dollars interact with their private bank. HESTA — Australia

Future Planner: retirement advice at scale for 700,000 members

AKQA built Future Planner for HESTA — the Health Employees' Superannuation Trust Australia — an interactive online retirement planning tool addressing a fund where women retire with 47% less super than men. The platform expanded advice-reach from under 1% to 98.5% of membership, with 133,187+ members using it since launch and 103,930+ unique retirement projections generated.
