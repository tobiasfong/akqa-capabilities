The Opportunity

Wealth management spans a wide range — from private bank clients to first-time investors. Each needs different tools.

At one end, high-net-worth clients with $2–200 million expect multi-channel execution and personalised advisory from their relationship managers. At the other, millions of young investors in Southeast Asia want simple, mobile-first access to stocks and crypto. Both need well-designed digital platforms — just very different ones.

For UOB, we built a wealth ecosystem with front-stage tools for clients and backstage tools for bankers across ASEAN. For Bank of Singapore, we designed RM navigator and pitchbook tools for private bank advisory. For WeInvest, a turn-key wealth platform that handles everything from regulatory compliance to operational journeys. And for MIDF and Nanovest, mobile investment apps aimed at everyday investors in Malaysia and Indonesia.

What connects all of this is AKQA's approach: we start by understanding how people actually invest and how advisors actually work, then design and build platforms that fit those real behaviours.

Case Studies
