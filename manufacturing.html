The Opportunity

Where intelligent systems meet physical production, everything accelerates.

Manufacturing is undergoing its deepest transformation since electrification. From construction materials in North America to consumer electronics in Japan to industrial tyre manufacturing in India, producers face the same challenge — legacy systems and fragmented digital presences that cannot keep pace with global markets. The companies that build intelligent, connected platforms will define the next generation of industrial efficiency and brand reach.

AKQA's APAC presence — anchored by the Tokyo studio — gives industrial and manufacturing brands a creative partner that understands regional markets without sacrificing global craft. The Sony BRAVIA campaign, led from Tokyo in cross-studio collaboration with Denmark and Paris, demonstrates how product storytelling developed in Japan can connect with audiences worldwide. Bridgestone in India and Breville in Australia reflect the same principle: precise, market-aware digital work that travels.

AKQA brings experience design thinking to industrial environments — from large-scale content platforms like Amrize's 300-page Adobe Experience Manager launch to IoT interfaces for Siemens Smart Spaces. The approach connects digital product craft with operational technology, creating systems that operators trust and organisations can scale across markets from Tokyo to Toronto.

What We Do
01
IoT Platform Design
For Siemens Smart Spaces, we designed an IoT platform for smart buildings that processes sensor data to optimize energy, occupancy, and environmental conditions. The platform achieved #1 ranking for IoT workplace solutions and 500% growth in user adoption after the Comfy acquisition.
02
Content Platform
For Amrize, we engineered a content-rich platform with 300+ pages and 40+ custom components on Adobe Experience Manager. The site achieved exceptional Lighthouse performance scores and demonstrates how complex industrial sites can be built for scale and usability.
03
Brand Identity Redesign
For Certhon — a 125-year-old horticultural technology company — we unified plants and AI through a brand identity that represents "When plants and AI grow as one." The visual system helps them communicate sustainable food cultivation to global markets.
04
Global Logistics Film
For DSV — a global logistics leader — we produced films capturing their operations across Japan, USA, Norway, South Africa, and Denmark. The work demonstrates how complex supply chain stories can be visualized for customers and stakeholders worldwide.
05
Partnerships & Growth
Siemens Smart Spaces has scaled through partnerships with Salesforce and SAP, bringing enterprise-grade integrations to facility management. The platform continues to lead the IoT workplace intelligence category with continued user growth and market adoption.
