The Opportunity

Where intelligent systems meet physical production, everything accelerates.

Manufacturing is undergoing its deepest transformation since electrification. From consumer electronics in Japan to industrial tyre manufacturing in India to home appliances in Australia, producers face the same challenge — legacy systems and fragmented digital presences that cannot keep pace with regional and global markets. The companies that build intelligent, connected platforms will define the next generation of industrial efficiency and brand reach.

AKQA's APAC presence — anchored by the Tokyo studio — gives industrial and manufacturing brands a creative partner that understands regional markets without sacrificing global craft. The Sony BRAVIA campaign, led from Tokyo, demonstrates how product storytelling developed in Japan can connect with audiences worldwide. Bridgestone in India, Breville in Australia, and Dyson in Singapore reflect the same principle: precise, market-aware digital work that travels.

AKQA brings experience design thinking to industrial environments — from cinematic brand platforms for Sony to digital product experiences for Dyson and Breville, to industrial brand transformation for Bridgestone and Apollo Tyres across the subcontinent. The approach connects digital product craft with operational technology, creating systems that consumers trust and organisations can scale across Asia-Pacific markets from Tokyo to Sydney to Mumbai.
