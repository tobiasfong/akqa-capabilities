The Opportunity

Every meal becomes a moment. Every moment becomes the brand.

Across Asia Pacific, grocery brands face a compounding challenge: consumers want to eat better but lack the time, budget, and guidance to do it consistently. From the Philippines to New Zealand, the expectation is shifting — families no longer want to browse aisles. They want intelligent companions that understand their kitchens, budgets, and nutritional goals.

AI-native grocery experiences replace every static touchpoint. The recipe page becomes a reasoning engine. The shopping list becomes a personalised demand signal. Budgeting tools become persistent advisors that learn household patterns over time. Conversational commerce — through text or voice — transforms brand catalogues into tailored guidance that drives basket size, loyalty, and nutritional outcomes.

AKQA builds the experience layer that connects grocery brands to families. From Nestlé's Goodnes platform — launched first in the Philippines and expanding across Asia — to immersive brand campaigns for Coca-Cola and Fonterra, the work spans conversational AI, sensory digital experiences, and regional market activation at scale.

What We Do
01
AI Meal Planning Platform
For Nestlé's Goodnes platform, we built an AI-powered companion powered by GPT-4.0. The assistant helps families plan meals, manage budgets, and create shopping lists. Personalised to household size, dietary preferences, and cost — Goodnes launched in the Philippines and is expanding across Asia, Africa, and Europe.
02
Campaign Platforms
For Coca-Cola, we created immersive brand campaigns including "A Brighter Way" for Pakistan and Ramadan activations. These experiences generated 8 million meals donated, 70% brand love score, 86% campaign recall, and 900 million TikTok views.
03
Conversational Commerce
Through Goodnes, we built text and voice interfaces that let consumers interact with 200+ Nestlé brands through a single AI companion. Three virtual assistants turn product discovery into dialogue and drive purchase decisions across mobile and voice channels.
04
Premium Chocolate Experience
For Whittaker's, we created an immersive digital experience that translates the sensory qualities of premium chocolate into a web experience. The design achieved 1.8 million views since launch, 20,000 new subscribers, and 50% rise in visitor uptake.
05
Regional Activation
For Coca-Cola across multiple markets, we designed culturally adapted campaigns including returnable bottle initiatives and Ramadan-specific experiences. Each platform maintains global brand consistency while adapting to local community values and behaviors.

Case Studies
Nestlé

AI-powered family companion launched first in the Philippines

AKQA partnered with Nestlé and Microsoft to create Goodnes — an AI-powered family companion that unifies hundreds of brands into a single destination for personalised meal planning, budgeting, and grocery lists. Powered by GPT-4.0 with three virtual companions, the platform launched in the Philippines and is expanding across Asia, Africa, and Europe.
Coca-Cola

Immersive brand campaigns spanning culture, sound, and sustainability

AKQA delivered a portfolio of campaigns for Coca-Cola across multiple markets — from "A brighter way" and Coke SoundZ to the Returnable Bottle programme and Ramadan activations. Each project translates brand values into culturally tuned digital experiences that drive engagement, trial, and measurable behaviour change.
Whittaker's

A digital experience you can taste

AKQA New Zealand created an immersive digital experience for Whittaker's that translates the sensory qualities of chocolate into a web experience. The project demonstrates how premium grocery brands can build emotional connection and drive trial through digital-first storytelling.
