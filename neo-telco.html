The Opportunity

What if connectivity was just the foundation, not the product?

Born-digital telco operators start with a competitive advantage: zero legacy. No physical retail stores. No billing systems to migrate. No enterprise infrastructure to maintain. They start digital, stay digital, and compete on experience, community, and platform economics — not infrastructure. This model is playing out across Asia-Pacific, from Southeast Asia's mobile-first youth markets to India's 1 billion+ subscriber base.

A neo telco is a super-app ecosystem. Connectivity fuels eLearning, gaming, entertainment, marketplace, and financial services. The telco becomes the foundation of daily digital life, generating economic activity far beyond minutes and data plans. AKQA has built this playbook from zero — by.U was designed and engineered from scratch for Indonesia's youth. The Telkomsel Super App connects 170 million customers across entertainment and e-commerce. Circles X unified martech and telco-tech into a platform for born-digital operators across Singapore and beyond. In India, AKQA works with Google/Jio, one of the region's most ambitious digital connectivity plays. And in the Pacific, Spark NZ's digital-first transformation — rebuilding their app to the point where positive reviews doubled — is the kind of experience-led shift that defines what modern telco can be.

Neo telcos win by moving fast, building culture, and obsessing over community. AKQA brings human-centred design, React Native engineering, and cross-functional squads covering research, UX, marketing, and data. The result: asset-light operators competing at national scale across Asia-Pacific.
