The Opportunity

Insurance in Asia is moving from paperwork to platforms. AKQA builds the experiences that make it work.

Across Thailand, Vietnam, Indonesia, Singapore, and Hong Kong, insurance companies are rethinking how customers buy, manage, and claim on their policies. The old model — complex documents, adversarial claims, fragmented portals — is giving way to mobile platforms that unify the entire journey.

For Muang Thai Life in Thailand, we built an end-to-end digital ecosystem covering purchase, telemedicine, personal finance, goal management, rewards, and claims. For Generali in Vietnam, we localised the global brand and designed habit-driven wellness tools. For AIA Thailand, we provided concept design and research for a health scanning app. For Thomson Medical, we consolidated 17 websites into a single patient experience.

Each project starts with understanding local attitudes toward insurance and healthcare. AKQA runs design thinking workshops, conduct user research, and build prototypes that are tested with real users before development begins.

Case Studies
Muang Thai Life

Total digital ecosystem for Thailand's leading insurer

AKQA partnered with Muang Thai Life to deliver an end-to-end digital ecosystem spanning straight-through purchase, telemedicine, personal finance assistance, goal management, lifestyle rewards, and claims — across mobile, desktop, and web. The engagement humanised the insurance experience for Thai customers through unified digital touchpoints.
Generali (GenVita)

Simplifying insurance through habit-building in Vietnam

AKQA built GenVita as more than a communication platform — we designed it to be a companion, advisor, and guide. The team localised Generali's global brand for Vietnam, created habit-driven wellness roadmaps, and streamlined the digital insurance process by overcoming technical and operational blockers.
Thomson Medical

Consolidating seventeen care properties into one experience

Through co-creation workshops and design thinking, AKQA helped Thomson Medical merge seventeen websites into a single digital experience. The project elevated brand awareness, improved customer satisfaction, and redesigned the overall service journey through the power of CX and digital integration.
