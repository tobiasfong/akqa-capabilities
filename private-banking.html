The Opportunity

Private banking clients expect digital tools that match the quality of their advisory relationship.

High-net-worth clients interact with their bank differently. They may trade directly, ask their relationship manager to execute, or have their family office handle it. They expect personalised advice, real-time portfolio visibility, and the ability to act through whichever channel suits them. The digital experience needs to support all of this.

AKQA designed these tools for Bank of Singapore — understanding how clients with $2–200 million in assets actually use their private bank, then building an RM navigator, pitchbook platform, and multi-channel execution system that fits how they work. For UOB, we built a wealth ecosystem with front-stage tools for clients and backstage tools for bankers across Southeast Asia.

Our financial services practice extends across Asia-Pacific. AKQA works with Prudential in Japan, where we bring the same rigour around complex financial products and client trust to the Japanese market. In Australia, we partnered with HESTA — a superannuation fund representing 700,000 members — to build Future Planner, a financial planning tool. In New Zealand, we built Evince for Partners Life, a digital insurance advice platform. These relationships inform how we approach wealth and private banking across the region.

This is a focused practice — clients where we go deep into understanding the advisory relationship and build tools that genuinely improve how financial professionals serve their clients.

What We Do
01
RM Navigator
A tool that helps relationship managers navigate client portfolios and lending details. For Bank of Singapore, we designed data charts that let RMs quickly scan financial reports and make better decisions for their clients.
02
Pitchbook Tools
Investment presentation tools that relationship managers use in client meetings. Combines portfolio data, market context, and client history so RMs can have more informed advisory conversations.
03
Multi-Channel Execution
Clients can place orders directly, ask their RM to execute, or have their family office handle trades. AKQA designed these three channels as a single experience for Bank of Singapore — matching how wealthy clients actually work.
04
Wealth Ecosystem
For UOB, we built a connected system — client-facing advisory tools on the front end, and portfolio management and optimisation tools for bankers on the back end. This covers wealth clients across Southeast Asia, part of AKQA's broader Asia-Pacific FSI footprint spanning Japan, Australia, and New Zealand.
