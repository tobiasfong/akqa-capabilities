The Opportunity

Corporate banking tools should be as good as the relationships they support.

Corporate banking still depends on relationships, but the tools behind those relationships often lag. Relationship managers work across fragmented systems to assess risk, structure lending, and manage client portfolios. Treasury teams rely on static reports while decisions need to happen faster. The gap between information and action costs time and money — and it plays out across every major market in Asia-Pacific.

AKQA closes that gap by designing better platforms. For DBS, that meant a unified workspace where relationship managers see client financials, lending details, and risk profiles in one place. For HSBC, it meant embedded banking — letting their customers complete banking transactions directly within partner platforms like accounting software, across 62 countries. In Japan, AKQA works with both MUFG and American Express — two of the country's most significant financial institutions — extending AKQA's corporate banking footprint into one of Asia's largest and most sophisticated markets.

AKQA's approach is research-led. AKQA interviews users, maps service blueprints, and builds design systems that scale globally. For Standard Chartered, a partnership spanning 16+ years and 22+ projects produced a design system with 44 reusable components used by 40 designers — the foundation that keeps their corporate banking products consistent across 45 countries.

What We Do
01
Operations Dashboards
AKQA designs dashboards that give treasury and operations teams a clear, real-time view of their business. For HSBC, this meant management dashboards that enable informed decision-making and streamlined operations across their platforms business.
02
Relationship Manager Tools
AKQA builds workspaces that help relationship managers serve clients better. For DBS, we designed a platform that brings client financials, lending details, and risk profiles into one view — plus a mobility solution that lets RMs evaluate risk and suggest solutions using data analytics.
03
Embedded Banking
AKQA helps banks bring their services into partner platforms, so customers can bank where they already work. For HSBC, this means users can make payments and borrow credit directly within external platforms like accounting software — across 62 countries.
04
Business Banking for SMEs
AKQA designs digital banking tools for business owners across Asia-Pacific. For Permata Bank, that meant smart, contextualised cash flow management for large enterprises. For Techcombank, a research-driven platform tailored to Vietnam's growing business landscape.
05
Design Systems at Global Scale
AKQA creates design systems that keep corporate banking products consistent across countries and teams — from Southeast Asia to Japan. For Standard Chartered, 44 reusable components and 11 documented patterns are now used by 40 designers — ensuring quality scales without slowing down.

Case Studies
HSBC

Embedding financial services where forty million customers already operate

AKQA partnered with HSBC to introduce embedded financing — enabling banking services on non-financial partner platforms across 62 countries. Users complete transactions from payments to credit borrowing as part of end-to-end experiences, such as paying invoices directly from accounting platforms.
