The Opportunity

How do incumbents compete when customers expect a digital-first experience?

Legacy operators have scale, subscriber loyalty, and infrastructure advantage. What they lacked: a digital-first mindset. Incumbents across APAC — from Indonesia to New Zealand — were losing ground to pure-play apps and digital upstarts. The challenge wasn't technology — it was architecture and organizational velocity.

AKQA designed and built the Telkomsel Super App, connecting 170 million subscribers across Southeast Asia. And when Spark, New Zealand's largest telco, needed to rebuild its app from the ground up — modernizing its customer experience end to end — AKQA led the digital strategy, app redesign, brand identity evolution, design system, prototyping, UX/UI, and in-app chat. The result doubled positive app store reviews and achieved full accessibility compliance.

The playbook holds across APAC: ecosystem architecture, subscriber lifecycle marketing, digital brand evolution, legacy integration. Whether the operator has 170 million subscribers or is the dominant player in a single market, incumbents who commit to continuous modernization can compete and win.
