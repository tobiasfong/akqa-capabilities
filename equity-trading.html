The Opportunity

Investing platforms need to work for institutional traders, active CFD traders, and first-time users alike — just in very different ways.

Our trading and investment work spans the full breadth of Asia-Pacific. For OSL, we designed a crypto exchange for institutional investors — defining the target audience and building an end-to-end experience across multiple channels and use cases. For Nanovest in Indonesia, we built the first app offering both crypto and global stocks, going from design to launch in three months.

For MIDF in Malaysia, we designed and launched a Shariah-compliant trading platform from the ground up — ETFs and stocks with integrated market insights, built for everyday citizens who had never traded before. For Cake DeFi, we reimagined the brand to reach beyond its crypto-native core, making decentralised finance accessible to newcomers.

Our APAC footprint extends to Australia's competitive online trading market, where we work with Pepperstone and Eightcap — two of Australia's leading CFD and forex brokers. These relationships reflect the depth of AKQA's capital markets practice: from first-time retail investors in Southeast Asia to sophisticated active traders using leveraged products in mature, heavily regulated markets.

What these projects share is AKQA's approach: user research first, then brand and design work, then engineering and integration. Every platform is designed around how its specific users actually invest.

Case Studies
OSL

Bringing new asset classes to institutional investors worldwide

AKQA worked closely with OSL to define the target audience for the crypto exchange and design the experience to match. Together, the team conceptualised an end-to-end experience to serve institutional investors across multiple channels and use cases.
MIDF Invest

From zero to trade in emerging markets

AKQA built MIDF Invest's first financial investment app — transforming a grant provider known for funding Malaysia's SME backbone into a digital-native trading platform. The all-in-one app provides Shariah-compliant stock and ETF trading with integrated market insights, identified through market gap analysis and customer pain point research.
Cake DeFi

The winning brand formula for decentralised finance

AKQA reimagined Cake DeFi's brand expression to reach beyond its crypto-native core. Customer research, lean-branding exercises, and journey workshops produced a new visual identity — anchored by the iconic HyperBerry colour palette — with brand guidelines, image assets, and a system designed to welcome newcomers to decentralised finance.
