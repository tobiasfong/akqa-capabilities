The Opportunity

Every journey begins before departure. The experience should start there too.

Travel and hospitality brands compete on experience — but the digital touchpoint is often the weakest link. From booking to check-in to post-stay engagement, guests and travellers expect the same attention to detail online that they receive in person. The gap between physical excellence and digital mediocrity is where loyalty is won or lost.

Intelligent platforms close that gap. AI-powered companions transform meal planning into inspiration, route planning into discovery, and booking into a relationship. Multi-brand digital ecosystems consolidate fragmented experiences into a single, intuitive journey — from the moment a guest imagines their trip through departure and beyond.

AKQA builds the digital experience layer for travel and hospitality across the globe. In North America, AKQA is Delta Air Lines' digital agency of record. In APAC, AKQA has partnered with Tourism Victoria in Australia, Tātaki Auckland Unlimited in New Zealand, and Nike Japan — whose Tokyo Marathon campaign mobilised 66 million media impressions and achieved the highest Nike footwear market share ever recorded at the race. Customer journey mapping, user personas, and personalised booking flows form the foundation. From airline apps that become primary self-service channels to major international sporting events that define a destination — the work is live, serving billions of journeys.

What We Do
01
Airline App Design
For Delta Air Lines, we built the Fly Delta app from launch in 2009 through continuous redesign. AKQA introduced Apple Pay, RFID baggage tracking, and contextual features that turned a boarding pass app into the primary self-service channel. Over 1 billion visits annually.
02
Hotel Website Redesign
For Centara, we rebuilt their multi-brand hotel website through customer journey mapping and user research. AKQA designed simplified booking flows, personalised rate pages, and destination guides that increased conversion and guest satisfaction across their global portfolio.
03
Luxury Launch Platforms
For Jumeirah's Marsa Al Arab, we designed a digital experience that captured the property's scale and prestige. The platform achieved fastest opening room occupancy in Jumeirah's history, earning coverage in Hollywood Reporter, Dezeen, and CNN.
04
Cruise Platform Redesign
For Ponant, we delivered a headless CMS-powered platform in under 10 months. The new site achieved 90% improvement in mobile performance, 83% reduction in product time-to-market, and better conversion through destination-specific storytelling.
05
Pilgrimage Platform
For Nusuk, we designed a digital platform serving 2 billion potential Hajj and Umrah pilgrims. The platform simplifies registration, personalises pilgrim journeys, and manages the world's largest seasonal migration event through intelligent, culturally respectful design.
