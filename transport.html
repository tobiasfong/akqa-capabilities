The Opportunity

The city is a mobility platform. Every journey is a system.

Transport systems — from military logistics to commercial aviation to urban mobility to automotive retail — are defined by manual coordination, siloed workflows, and fragmented visibility. The gap between operations and experience costs time, fuel, and trust. Intelligent platforms that connect people, vehicles, and data in real time can close that gap entirely.

A unified transport management system replaces paper-based indenting with digital coordination. Mobile-optimised platforms ensure soldiers, passengers, and logistics officers can execute services from any device. Real-time status tracking creates transparency across roles — from ground-level operators to command-level decision makers.

AKQA builds the experience layer for transport operations across the Asia-Pacific. From world-class military logistics platforms built with ST Engineering and SMRT, to airline applications integrated with Amadeus, to immersive automotive experiences in Tokyo — the work spans defence, aviation, urban mobility, and brand experience. Clients including Transurban in Australia, Waka Kotahi in New Zealand, and Japan's JR East and JAL reflect the breadth of transport categories and markets AKQA serves across the region.

What We Do
01
Defence Logistics Platforms
MINDEF needed unified management of vehicle acquisition, assignment, and tracking across military operations. AKQA partnered with ST Engineering and SMRT to build a system that replaced manual processes with mobile-optimised coordination — giving soldiers and operations staff transparency and alignment across all transport roles.
02
Aviation Efficiency Systems
Singapore Airlines and Rolls Royce partnered with AKQA to bring data intelligence to pilot decision-making. The system combines in-flight analytics with design and data science to nudge pilots toward more fuel-efficient flights — improving safety, reducing environmental impact, and enhancing operational outcomes.
03
Airport Experience Platforms
Seletar Airport and Malaysia Airport partnered with AKQA to reimagine the airport journey from home to gate. Built on Adobe Experience Manager and optimised through extensive usability testing, these platforms serve commercial passengers and business users with personalised, intuitive experiences.
04
Airline Mobile Solutions
Garuda Airlines partnered with AKQA to lead strategy, design, and development of a fully integrated mobile app with Amadeus booking and loyalty engine. The platform ensures the airline brand is present at every touchpoint — from search through booking, loyalty, and arrival.
05
Urban Mobility Systems
MINDEF's NS Mobility system connects individuals to national service through integrated logistics and fleet management. AKQA designed a people-first approach that reimagines how soldiers and operations staff access transport resources — making urban mobility within defence operations seamless and efficient.

Case Studies
MINDEF

World-class transport management for Singapore's national defence

Together with ST Engineering and SMRT, AKQA designed and optimised transport workflows for MINDEF — creating a holistic transport management system that assists soldiers in acquiring required resources for missions and training. The one-stop platform replaced manual processes with digital coordination, visibility, and mobile access.
Singapore Airlines & Rolls Royce

Pilot efficiency and safety through in-flight data intelligence

AKQA partnered with Singapore Airlines and Rolls Royce to push the boundaries of in-flight data. AKQA brought design, data science, and technology together to nudge pilots into more fuel-efficient flights — improving safety and reducing environmental impact.
Volvo

Volvo Studio Tokyo Explorer — reaching a new generation of Japanese drivers

AKQA created an immersive companion app for Volvo's studio in Tokyo's Aoyama district, designed to attract younger Japanese audiences less interested in traditional car ownership. AR-triggered interactions, Virtual Northern Lights, a floating zen garden, virtual Stockholm driving, and an AR-enhanced Fika Café turned the physical space into a living digital experience. Visitor numbers and test drives both doubled within the first 6 months. "It was a delight working with AKQA to create a truly unique, fun and interactive experience for our space and successfully attract a new audience." — Miyuki Kitagawa, Brand Manager, Volvo Japan
