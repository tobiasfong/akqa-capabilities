The Opportunity

The car is no longer the product. The experience around it is.

The automotive industry is being remade by electrification, software-defined vehicles, and new ownership models. Across Asia-Pacific — from Hyundai in Korea to NIO in China to Nissan and Toyota in Japan — customers expect digital experiences that match the sophistication of the vehicles themselves. Static brochure sites and transactional configurators are no longer enough.

Digital showrooms replace physical footfall with narrative-driven journeys. AI companions transform the showroom into a conversational interface. Immersive activations at motor shows create experiences that outperform every other booth on the floor. Every touchpoint — from configurator to showroom app to ownership experience — becomes an opportunity to deepen the relationship between driver and brand.

AKQA builds the experience layer that connects automotive brands to their customers across Asia-Pacific. In Japan, AKQA partnered with Nissan at the Tokyo Motor Show to deliver the world's first VR car configurator — drawing 70-minute queue times — and with Volvo Japan to double visitor numbers and test drives at the Volvo Studio Tokyo in Aoyama. In China, AKQA built an AI voice assistant for General Motors that achieved a 76% conversation rate. In India, AKQA has worked with Volkswagen UK on digital experience and transformation. In Australia, AKQA's partnership with Volvo and IKEA produced The Swedish Test Drive — a gamified EV experience combining IKEA's iconic road rug with Volvo's electric lineup, Gemini maps integration, and collectible wooden toy cars. The result: brands that sell not just the drive but the world around it — across showrooms, screens, and city streets from Tokyo to Sydney to Mumbai.

What We Do
01
Immersive Motor Show Activation — Tokyo
For Nissan at the Tokyo Motor Show, AKQA created the world's first VR car configuration experience in partnership with Oculus Rift. The IDx stations drew average sessions of 7 minutes and queue wait times of up to 70 minutes — outperforming every other activity at the show. The installation won First Place at the IAB Creative Showcase in the Digital Installations category.
02
Immersive Showroom — Tokyo
For Volvo Japan, AKQA created an immersive companion app for the Volvo Studio Tokyo in Aoyama. AR-triggered interactions, Virtual Northern Lights, a floating zen garden, and an AR-enhanced Fika Café turned the physical space into a living digital experience — doubling visitor numbers and test drives within 6 months.
03
AI In-Showroom Experience — China
For General Motors in China, we built "Hello Billy" — an AI voice assistant with holographic display and Kinect sensors. The multi-sensory experience achieved 76% conversation rate and 100% touch interaction rate with visitors.
04
AI Vehicle Companion — China
For NIO, AKQA helped shape the Banyan intelligent vehicle system and NOMI — the smart AI companion that transforms the in-cabin experience. The system integrates voice interaction, personalised driving preferences, and connected services into a seamless ownership journey for Chinese EV drivers.
05
Digital Retail & Campaign Platforms — Australia, New Zealand & India
Across Australia and New Zealand, AKQA works with Mitsubishi, Nissan, and Volvo on digital retail experiences suited to local market needs. In Australia, AKQA's Volvo × IKEA "Swedish Test Drive" combined IKEA's iconic road rug with Volvo's EV lineup — a gamified experience with Gemini maps integration and collectible wooden toy cars. In India, AKQA has worked with Volkswagen UK on automotive digital experience and transformation.
